# Setting Up Your Account <span class="badge-all">All Contributors</span>

Before you can start contributing, you'll need to activate your account and log in. This takes about two minutes.

---

## 1. Check Your Email

Look for an email from PATHS with the subject line **"Set Your Password"**. This is sent to the address your Limitless contact provided during onboarding.

![Password setup email in your inbox](../assets/screenshots/email-set-password.png)

!!! warning "Can't find the email?"
    Check your spam or junk folder. If it's not there, contact your Limitless point of contact to have the invitation resent.

## 2. Set Your Password

Click the link in the email. You'll be taken to a page where you can choose a strong password for your account.

![Set your password screen](../assets/screenshots/set-password-screen.png)

## 3. Log In

Go to **[paths.limitless-longevity.health](https://paths.limitless-longevity.health)**. Enter your email address and the password you just created.

![PATHS login screen](../assets/screenshots/login-screen.png)

## 4. Set Up Your Profile

Once logged in, add your name, a professional photo, and a short bio. This helps your team recognize you and appears alongside your published content.

Click your initials in the top-right corner, then select **Profile** to edit your details.

![Profile setup screen](../assets/screenshots/profile-setup.png)

---

!!! tip "Bookmark for quick access"
    Save **paths.limitless-longevity.health** to your browser bookmarks so you can get back in with one click.
