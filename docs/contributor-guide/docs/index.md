# Welcome to PATHS

Welcome to the **PATHS** contributor platform by Limitless Longevity Consultancy. This is where you'll create, review, and publish world-class longevity and health content for our members.

We've designed your workspace to make contributing effortless — so you can focus on what you do best: sharing your expertise.

This guide walks you through everything you need to get started and publish your first piece of content.

---

## Your Role

You've been invited to PATHS in one of two roles. Your role determines what you can do on the platform.

<div class="role-cards" markdown>
<div class="role-card" markdown>
### Author
You create content. As a coach, physician, or subject-matter expert, you write articles and submit them for review. An editor on the Limitless team will review your work and handle publishing — you never need to worry about the technical side.
</div>
<div class="role-card" markdown>
### Editor / Publisher
You review and publish content. As part of the internal Limitless team, you review submitted articles, provide feedback, approve or request changes, and publish finished pieces to the platform. You can also create your own content.
</div>
</div>
