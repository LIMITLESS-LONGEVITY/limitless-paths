# Taking Quizzes

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

Quizzes on the PATHS platform are embedded directly within articles as interactive blocks. They are designed to reinforce key concepts and help you retain what you have learned.

## Where to Find Quizzes

Quizzes appear inline within article content. As you read through an article, you may encounter a quiz block that tests your understanding of the material covered in that section. Not every article contains a quiz, but many of the core educational articles do.

<Callout type="info">
  Quizzes are placed contextually within articles, right after the content they relate to. This helps reinforce concepts while they are fresh.
</Callout>

## How Quizzes Work

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Read through the article content until you reach a quiz block.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Review the question and the available answer choices. Quizzes use a **multiple choice** format.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Select the answer you believe is correct.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Submit your answer to receive **immediate feedback** indicating whether you answered correctly.</Step>
  <Step number={5}>If your answer was incorrect, review the explanation provided and consider re-reading the relevant section.</Step>
</StepList>

## AI-Generated Quizzes

Some quizzes on the platform are generated using AI based on the article's content. This allows the platform to provide relevant, targeted questions that directly test comprehension of the material. AI-generated quizzes go through an editorial review process to ensure accuracy and quality.

<Callout type="tip">
  Do not worry about getting a wrong answer. Quizzes are a learning tool, not a graded exam. Getting a question wrong and reading the explanation is one of the most effective ways to learn.
</Callout>

## Quiz Results

Quiz responses provide immediate feedback per question. There is no aggregate quiz score or pass/fail threshold. The goal is to support active learning by prompting you to engage with the material rather than passively reading.

## Quizzes in Courses vs. Articles

Quizzes can appear in both standalone articles and within course lessons. The format and interaction are the same in both contexts. In courses, completing a quiz does not affect your lesson completion status — you mark lessons complete separately using the **Mark as Complete** button.
