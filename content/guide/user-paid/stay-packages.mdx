# Longevity Stay Packages

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

PATHS offers immersive **Longevity Stay Packages** at El Fuerte Marbella, combining luxury hospitality with hospital-grade diagnostics at Recoletas Salud. Your stay is enhanced with PATHS content — pre-arrival preparation, daily protocols during your stay, and long-term follow-up.

## Available Packages

Navigate to `/stays` in the main navigation to browse three packages:

| Package | Duration | Price (Member) | Follow-up |
|---------|----------|---------------|-----------|
| **3-Day Discovery** | 3 nights | €2,800 | 1 month |
| **5-Day Immersion** | 5 nights | €5,200 | 3 months |
| **7-Day Transformation** | 7 nights | €7,600 | 6 months |

Each package includes accommodation, diagnostic assessments, coaching sessions, and personalized protocols. The 5-Day Immersion is marked as **Most Popular**.

## What's Included

All packages include:

- Accommodation at El Fuerte Marbella
- Diagnostic assessment at Recoletas Salud
- Coaching sessions (movement, nutrition, and more)
- Personalized longevity protocol development
- Follow-up teleconsultation period (varies by package)

Higher-tier packages add spa access, daily personalized training, advanced therapies (IV, cryotherapy, hyperbaric), and longer follow-up periods.

## Time-Gated Content During Your Stay

Stay programs use PATHS courses with daily content that unlocks progressively:

- **Modules = Days** — Each day of your stay has a dedicated module
- **Lessons = Activities** — Each activity block (morning session, afternoon workshop, etc.) is a lesson
- Content is **time-gated** — Day 3 content unlocks on Day 3 of your stay, not before

If you try to access a future day's content, you'll see a **"Available on Day X"** message.

## Stay-Aware AI Protocols

During your stay, the daily protocol widget on your [dashboard](/guide/user-paid/dashboard) is enhanced with stay-specific context. It knows your current day, location, and scheduled activities, and generates protocols that complement your in-person program.
