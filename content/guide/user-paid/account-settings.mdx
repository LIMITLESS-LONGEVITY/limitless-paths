# Account Settings

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

Your account settings let you manage your profile information, update your password, and control your account preferences. This guide covers the key settings available to you.

## Accessing Account Settings

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Click your avatar in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Select **Account** from the dropdown menu.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>You will see your profile information and available settings.</Step>
</StepList>

<Screenshot src="user-paid/account-settings-overview.png" alt="Account settings page showing profile information and available options" caption="The Account page provides access to your profile, email, password, and billing settings." />

## Updating Your Display Name

Your display name appears on your profile and in platform interactions.

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>On the Account page, locate the **Name** field.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Enter your updated first name and last name.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click **Save** to apply the changes.</Step>
</StepList>

## Changing Your Email Address

You can update the email address associated with your account.

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>On the Account page, locate the **Email** field.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Enter your new email address.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click **Save**. A verification email will be sent to your new address.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Check your inbox and click the verification link to confirm the change.</Step>
</StepList>

<Callout type="warning">
  Your email address is used for login. After changing it, use the new email to sign in. The change will not take full effect until you verify the new address.
</Callout>

<Screenshot src="user-paid/account-email-password.png" alt="Account page showing email and password fields with Save buttons" caption="Update your email or password from the Account page." />

## Changing Your Password

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>On the Account page, locate the **Password** section.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Enter your current password for verification.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Enter your new password. Use a strong, unique password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Confirm the new password and click **Save**.</Step>
</StepList>

<Callout type="tip">
  If you have forgotten your current password, use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page to reset it via email.
</Callout>

## Email Verification

The platform uses email verification to confirm your identity. If you registered recently or changed your email, you may need to verify before accessing all features. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link provided. If you did not receive it, you can request a new verification email from the Account page.

<Screenshot src="user-paid/email-verification-prompt.png" alt="Email verification prompt on the Account page with resend button" caption="If your email is unverified, a prompt appears with an option to resend the verification email." />

Your account area includes several sub-pages, all accessible from the left sidebar:

| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|

## Logging Out

To log out of your account:

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Click your avatar in the top-right corner.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Select **Log out** from the dropdown menu.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>You will be redirected to the homepage. Your session is ended and you will need to sign in again to access your account.</Step>
</StepList>
