# Learning Streaks

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

PATHS tracks your learning consistency with a **streak system**. Every day you complete at least one lesson, your streak increases. The dashboard shows both your current streak and your all-time longest streak.

## How Streaks Work

- **Current Streak** — The number of consecutive days you've completed a lesson. Resets to 0 if you miss a day.
- **Longest Streak** — Your all-time record. Never resets — only grows when you beat it.
- **Last Activity Date** — The most recent day you completed a lesson (used for streak calculation).

Streaks update automatically when you mark a lesson as complete. The system uses UTC midnight for day boundaries, so completing multiple lessons in the same calendar day counts as one day of activity.

## Viewing Your Streak

Your current streak appears as a **flame icon** card on your [dashboard](/guide/user-paid/dashboard), alongside your other stats (Courses Enrolled, Lessons Completed, Courses Completed).

<Callout type="tip">
  Aim to complete at least one lesson per day — even a short 5-minute lesson counts. Consistency matters more than volume.
</Callout>
