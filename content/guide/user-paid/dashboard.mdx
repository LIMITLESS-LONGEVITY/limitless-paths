# Your Dashboard

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

Your dashboard at `/account` is your home base on PATHS. It gives you an at-a-glance view of your learning progress, daily protocols, enrolled courses, and recent activity.

## Dashboard Overview

When you log in, your dashboard displays:

- **Welcome message** with your name and tier badge (e.g., "Welcome back, Sarah" with a **PREMIUM** badge)
- **Stats cards** showing your key metrics
- **Today's Protocol** with actionable morning, afternoon, and evening routines
- **Continue Learning** with your enrolled courses and progress bars
- **Recent Activity** showing your latest lesson completions
- **Explore** links to browse courses, articles, and diagnostic packages

## Stats Cards

Four cards at the top of your dashboard track your key metrics:

| Card | What It Shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Courses Enrolled** | Total number of courses you've joined |
| **Lessons Completed** | Total lessons you've finished across all courses |
| **Courses Completed** | Courses where you've reached 100% |
| **Day Streak** | Your current learning streak (consecutive days with activity) |

<Callout type="tip">
  Complete at least one lesson per day to build your learning streak. Your longest streak is also tracked — see [Learning Streaks](/guide/user-paid/learning-streaks) for details.
</Callout>

## Today's Protocol

If you have a **Premium** or higher tier, the dashboard generates a personalized daily protocol based on your [Health Profile](/guide/user-paid/health-profile) and course progress. Protocols are divided into three time blocks:

- **Morning** — e.g., "10 min cold exposure," "Take Vitamin D with breakfast"
- **Afternoon** — e.g., "30 min Zone 2 cardio," "Mediterranean lunch prep"
- **Evening** — e.g., "Blue light cutoff by 8pm," "Magnesium glycinate 400mg"

You can check off each item as you complete it. The protocol regenerates daily with fresh recommendations.

## Continue Learning

This section shows your enrolled courses with:

- Course title and pillar badge
- A progress bar showing your completion percentage
- A direct link to resume where you left off

Click any course to jump straight to your next lesson.

## Onboarding Tour

The first time you visit your dashboard, a **Welcome to PATHS** tour modal walks you through the key features. You can skip it or step through all the highlights. The tour won't appear again after you dismiss it.
