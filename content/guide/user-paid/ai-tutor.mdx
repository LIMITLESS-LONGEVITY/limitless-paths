# Using the AI Tutor

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

The AI tutor is an intelligent assistant built into the PATHS platform. It answers your questions using content from the platform's knowledge base, helping you deepen your understanding of longevity science topics.

## How It Works

The AI tutor uses **retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)** to provide accurate, grounded answers. When you ask a question, the system:

1. Searches the platform's articles, lessons, and course materials for relevant content.
2. Retrieves the most relevant passages using semantic search and reranking.
3. Generates a response based on that retrieved content.

<Callout type="info" title="Grounded in PATHS content">
  The AI tutor's answers are based on content published on the PATHS platform, not general internet search results. This means responses are curated, vetted, and aligned with the platform's educational standards.
</Callout>

## Accessing the AI Tutor

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Open any article on the platform.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Look for the **AI Tutor** panel in the article sidebar.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Type your question in the input field and press **Send**.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>The tutor will respond with an answer drawn from relevant platform content.</Step>
</StepList>

## Asking Effective Questions

The AI tutor works best when your questions are specific and related to topics covered on the platform. For example:

- "What are the key biomarkers for metabolic health?"
- "How does sleep quality affect longevity outcomes?"
- "Explain the difference between biological age and chronological age."

General or off-topic questions may receive limited responses, since the tutor only draws from PATHS content.

<Screenshot src="user-paid/ai-tutor-question-example.png" alt="Example of a specific question getting a detailed response from the AI Tutor" caption="Specific questions about platform topics yield the most detailed responses." />

## Conversation Context

The AI tutor maintains context within your current conversation. You can ask follow-up questions that build on previous answers without repeating background information. Starting a new session begins a fresh conversation.

<Callout type="tip">
  Use the AI tutor while reading an article to clarify concepts or explore related topics in more depth. It is designed to complement the learning experience, not replace it.
</Callout>

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="How does RAG work behind the scenes?">
    When you ask a question, the system converts it into a semantic embedding and searches the platform's vector database for the most relevant content passages. These passages are then reranked for relevance and used as context for the AI to generate a grounded answer. This approach ensures responses are based on actual platform content rather than general knowledge.
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Can the AI Tutor access content above my tier?">
    No. The AI Tutor respects your subscription tier. It only retrieves and references content that your effective access level permits. Upgrading your plan gives the tutor a broader knowledge base to work with.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

## Content Access and the Tutor

The AI tutor respects your access level. It will only retrieve and reference content that your subscription tier grants access to. This means Premium users may receive richer answers than Regular users for topics covered in premium-tier articles.
