# Completing Lessons

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

Courses on PATHS are organized into modules, each containing a sequence of lessons. This guide covers how the lesson viewer works and how your progress is tracked.

## Lesson Viewer Layout

When you open a lesson, you will see two main areas:

- **Content area** — the main panel displaying the lesson content, which may include text, embedded videos, callouts, and interactive quiz blocks.
- **Course sidebar** — a navigation panel on the side showing all modules and lessons in the course, with your completion status for each.

The sidebar lets you jump to any lesson in the course without leaving the viewer.

<Screenshot src="user-paid/lesson-viewer-layout.png" alt="Lesson viewer showing content area and course sidebar with module navigation" caption="The lesson viewer with the content area on the left and course sidebar on the right." />

## Marking Lessons Complete

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Read through the lesson content in the main area.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>When you have finished, click the **Mark as Complete** button at the bottom of the lesson.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>The lesson will show a checkmark in the course sidebar, and the next lesson will become highlighted.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Click **Next Lesson** to continue, or select any other lesson from the sidebar.</Step>
</StepList>

<Screenshot src="user-paid/mark-complete-button.png" alt="Mark as Complete button at the bottom of a lesson" caption="Click Mark as Complete when you finish reading a lesson." />

<Callout type="info">
  You can mark lessons complete in any order. The platform does not enforce a strict sequence, though following the intended order is recommended for the best learning experience.
</Callout>

## Tracking Your Progress

The course sidebar displays a **progress bar** that fills as you complete lessons. A percentage is shown alongside it, calculated as the number of completed lessons divided by the total lessons in the course.

<Screenshot src="user-paid/progress-bar-sidebar.png" alt="Course sidebar showing progress bar with completion percentage" caption="The progress bar in the course sidebar updates as you complete lessons." />

Progress is tracked per course and is visible from:

- The **course sidebar** while you are in the lesson viewer
- The **course card** on the Courses page (for enrolled courses)
- Your **account page** under enrolled courses

## Resuming Where You Left Off

Your progress is saved automatically. When you return to a course, you can pick up exactly where you stopped:

- From the **Courses** page, click **Continue** on an enrolled course to jump to your most recent incomplete lesson.
- From the **course sidebar**, completed lessons are marked with checkmarks so you can quickly identify what remains.

<Callout type="tip">
  If you complete all lessons in a course, the progress bar will show 100%. The course will be marked as complete, a [certificate](/guide/user-paid/certificates) is generated automatically, and you can generate an [AI action plan](/guide/user-paid/action-plans) based on the course content.
</Callout>

## Learning Streaks

Each lesson you complete counts toward your [learning streak](/guide/user-paid/learning-streaks). Completing at least one lesson per day builds your consecutive-day streak, visible on your [dashboard](/guide/user-paid/dashboard).

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Can I un-complete a lesson?">
    Currently, lesson completions cannot be reversed. If you want to revisit a lesson, you can navigate back to it at any time — it will still be marked as complete, but you can re-read the content.
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Does completing lessons in order matter?">
    The platform does not enforce a strict sequence. However, course authors design the module and lesson order for progressive learning, so following the intended order is recommended.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>
