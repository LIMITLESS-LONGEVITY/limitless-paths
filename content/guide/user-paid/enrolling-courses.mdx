# Enrolling in Courses

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

As a paid subscriber, you have access to enroll in courses that match your subscription tier. This guide walks you through finding and enrolling in courses.

<Callout type="info" title="Paid plan required">
  Course enrollment is available to users on a Regular, Premium, or Enterprise plan. Free-tier users can browse course descriptions but cannot enroll.
</Callout>

## Browsing Available Courses

Navigate to the **Courses** page from the main navigation. You will see a listing of all courses on the platform, including:

<Screenshot src="user-paid/courses-listing.png" alt="Courses listing page showing available courses with tier badges" caption="The Courses page displays all available courses with their access tier and enrollment status." />

- Course title and description
- Number of modules and lessons
- Access level required (Regular, Premium, or Enterprise)
- Estimated completion time

Courses that fall within your subscription tier will show an **Enroll** button. Courses above your tier will display a locked indicator with information about which plan is required.

## How to Enroll

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Browse to the **Courses** page and find a course you want to take.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Click the course card to view its full description, module outline, and learning objectives.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click the **Enroll** button on the course page.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>You will see an enrollment confirmation. The course now appears in your enrolled courses list.</Step>
</StepList>

<Screenshot src="user-paid/course-detail-enroll.png" alt="Course detail page with Enroll button and module outline" caption="The course detail page shows the module outline and Enroll button." />

## Accessing Your Enrolled Courses

Once enrolled, you can access your courses in two ways:

- **Account page** — go to your account and find the enrolled courses section, which lists all courses you have joined.
- **Courses page** — enrolled courses will show a **Continue** button instead of the Enroll button, taking you directly to where you left off.

<Callout type="tip">
  After enrolling, you can start the first lesson immediately. Your progress is saved automatically, so you can return at any time and pick up where you left off.
</Callout>

<Screenshot src="user-paid/enrolled-courses-account.png" alt="Account page showing enrolled courses list with progress indicators" caption="Your enrolled courses appear on your account page with progress tracking." />

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="What happens if I enroll but never start the course?">
    Your enrollment is preserved indefinitely. The course will remain in your enrolled courses list with 0% progress. You can start it at any time. There is no enrollment expiration.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

## Enrollment and Access Levels

Your enrollment persists even if you change subscription tiers. However, if you downgrade to a tier below the course's access level, you will not be able to access lesson content until you upgrade again. Your progress is never lost.
