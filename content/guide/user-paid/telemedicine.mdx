# Telemedicine Consultations

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

PATHS connects you with the clinical team for expert health guidance via telemedicine. From biomarker interpretation to intervention protocols — get personalized advice without leaving home.

## Available Services

Navigate to `/telemedicine` to browse four consultation types:

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Biomarker Review** | Detailed analysis of your diagnostic results with personalized recommendations |
| **Health Planning** | Strategic longevity planning based on your goals, genetics, and lifestyle |
| **Medication Guidance** | Expert guidance on supplements, medications, and intervention protocols |
| **Follow-Up Care** | Ongoing monitoring and protocol adjustments after diagnostics or stays |

## Booking a Consultation

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Navigate to `/telemedicine`.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Scroll to the **Request a Consultation** form.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Fill in your name, email, phone, preferred topic, preferred date, and any additional information.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Click **Request Consultation**. The clinical team will follow up to schedule your session.</Step>
</StepList>

## AI Tutor Escalation

You don't always need to visit the telemedicine page directly. When using the [AI Tutor](/guide/user-paid/ai-tutor), if you ask a question that requires professional clinical judgment (e.g., medication decisions, symptom interpretation), the tutor may display a **gold consultation card** suggesting you book a session.

This card includes:

- A recommendation to speak with a clinician
- An **auto-prefilled booking form** — your name and email are pre-filled from your account, and the topic is derived from your conversation
- A direct link to the telemedicine page

<Callout type="info" title="Seamless handoff">
  The AI tutor is designed to recognize when questions exceed educational scope. The escalation is seamless — one click from the tutor panel to a pre-filled booking form.
</Callout>
