# AI Action Plans

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

When you complete a course on PATHS, the AI can generate a **personalized 30-day action plan** that translates what you learned into concrete daily actions — tailored to your [Health Profile](/guide/user-paid/health-profile).

## How Action Plans Work

Action plans are structured as a 4-week program with specific daily actions for each week. The AI generates the plan based on:

- The course content you just completed
- Your health goals and pillar priorities
- Your biomarker data and health conditions (if available)
- Your current medications (to avoid conflicts)

## Generating an Action Plan

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Complete all lessons in a course (reach 100% progress).</Step>
  <Step number={2}>On the course completion page, look for the **Generate Action Plan** button.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click the button. The AI will analyze the course content and your health data to create a personalized plan.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Your plan appears with a weekly breakdown of daily actions.</Step>
</StepList>

<Callout type="tip">
  Fill in your [Health Profile](/guide/user-paid/health-profile) before generating an action plan. Plans generated with health data are significantly more personalized than those without it.
</Callout>

## Viewing Your Action Plans

All your generated action plans are available at **Account** → **Action Plans** (or `/account/plans`). Each plan shows:

- The course it was generated from
- Date of generation
- A 4-week breakdown with expandable day-by-day actions

If you haven't completed any courses yet, you'll see a prompt to browse and enroll in one.
