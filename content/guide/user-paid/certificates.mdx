# Course Completion Certificates

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

<Callout type="warning" title="Coming Soon">
  Course completion certificates are a planned feature and are not yet available. This page describes what to expect when the feature launches.
</Callout>

## What Certificates Will Offer

Upon completing all lessons in a course, you will be able to generate a **PDF certificate** confirming your completion. Certificates will include:

- Your name
- The course title
- The date of completion
- A unique certificate identifier for verification

## How It Will Work

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Complete all lessons in a course (100% progress).</Step>
  <Step number={2}>A **Download Certificate** button will appear on the course page.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click the button to generate and download your certificate as a PDF.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Share the certificate on professional profiles, with employers, or for continuing education records.</Step>
</StepList>

## Shareable Credentials

Certificates will include a verification URL that allows third parties to confirm the authenticity of your completion. This makes them suitable for professional development records and employer verification.

<Callout type="info">
  Certificates will be available for all paid tiers. Any course you have fully completed will be eligible for a certificate, including courses completed before the feature launches.
</Callout>

## Stay Informed

We will announce the launch of certificates through the platform. In the meantime, continue completing courses — your progress is tracked and will be recognized once certificates are available.
