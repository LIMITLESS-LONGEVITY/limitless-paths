# Course Certificates

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

When you complete all lessons in a course, PATHS automatically generates a **completion certificate** — a branded, verifiable credential you can share with employers, on LinkedIn, or for continuing education records.

## How Certificates Work

Certificates are generated automatically when your course progress reaches **100%**. There is no manual step required — simply complete every lesson in a course.

Each certificate includes:

- Your full name
- The course title and content pillar
- The instructor's name
- Date of completion
- A unique certificate number (e.g., `PATHS-1711543200-X7K2`)

## Viewing Your Certificates

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Navigate to **Account** → **Certificates** in the sidebar.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>You'll see a list of all certificates you've earned, with course title, pillar, and completion date.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click any certificate to view the full branded display.</Step>
</StepList>

<Screenshot src="user-paid/certificates-list.png" alt="List of earned certificates in the account section" caption="Your certificates page showing all earned credentials." />

## Sharing & Printing

Each certificate has a **public shareable URL** at `/certificates/[id]`. You can:

- **Copy the link** and share it with anyone — no login required to view
- **Print** using the print button — the certificate is formatted for clean printing with `@media print` styles
- **Share on LinkedIn** or include in professional development portfolios

<Callout type="tip">
  Add your certificate URL to your LinkedIn profile under "Licenses & certifications" for professional visibility.
</Callout>

## Organization Certificates

If you're part of an organization on PATHS, your certificates are also visible to your team administrator for compliance tracking. See [Team Certificates](/guide/user-organization/team-certificates) for details.

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="What if I completed a course before certificates were available?">
    Certificates are generated retroactively. If you completed a course before the feature launched, your certificate was automatically created and is available in your Certificates page.
  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem title="Can I re-download a certificate?">
    Yes. Certificates are always available at their public URL and in your account. You can view, print, or share them at any time.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>
