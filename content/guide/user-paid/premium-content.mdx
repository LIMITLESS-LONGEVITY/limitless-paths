# Understanding Tiered Content Access

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

Content on the PATHS platform is organized into access tiers. Your subscription level determines which articles, courses, and resources you can view. This guide explains how the tiered system works.

## Access Tiers

The platform has four content access levels, ordered from most accessible to most exclusive:

| Tier | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Free** | Basic introductory content |
| **Regular** | All free content plus standard educational materials |
| **Premium** | All regular content plus advanced and in-depth resources |
| **Enterprise** | Full access to all platform content, including organization-specific materials |

Each piece of content is assigned an access level by the editorial team. Your subscription tier grants access to all content at your level **and below**.

## How Locked and Unlocked Content Appears

As you browse articles and courses, content is clearly marked:

- **Unlocked content** displays normally and can be opened, read, and interacted with fully.
- **Locked content** shows a restricted indicator with the tier required to access it. You can see the title and description, but the full content is not available until you upgrade.

<Callout type="info">
  Locked content is not hidden. You can always see what is available at higher tiers, so you know what you would gain by upgrading your plan.
</Callout>

## Organization Access

If you belong to an organization (such as a hospital, hotel group, or corporate wellness program), your organization may have its own access level. The platform uses a **"highest wins" union policy**:

- Your effective access level is whichever is higher — your personal subscription tier or your organization's access level.
- You do not need to choose between them. Both are evaluated, and the more permissive level applies.

<Callout type="tip" title="Example">
  If you have a Regular personal subscription but your organization has Premium access, you can view all Premium content. Conversely, if your personal plan is Premium but your organization is Regular, your Premium access still applies.
</Callout>

## Upgrading for More Content

If you encounter content that requires a higher tier, you can upgrade your subscription from the billing page. See the [Managing Your Subscription](/guide/user-paid/managing-subscription) guide for details on how to change your plan.

## AI Tutor and Access Levels

The AI tutor also respects access tiers. When generating answers, it only retrieves content that your effective access level permits. Upgrading your tier gives the AI tutor a broader knowledge base to draw from when answering your questions.
