# Booking Diagnostics

<RoleTag role="user-paid" />

PATHS connects your learning with real-world diagnostics. After completing courses on biomarkers, nutrition, or longevity science, you can book diagnostic packages at partner clinics to measure what you've learned about.

## Browsing Diagnostic Packages

Navigate to `/diagnostics` (linked from the **Explore** section of your dashboard or footer links). You'll find available diagnostic packages with:

- Package name and description
- Which biomarkers are included
- Pricing information
- What to expect during the appointment

<Callout type="info">
  Diagnostic packages are offered in partnership with Recoletas Salud Marbella. Availability may vary by location.
</Callout>

## Contextual Suggestions

As you complete courses and articles, PATHS suggests relevant diagnostic packages. For example, after finishing a course on metabolic health, you might see a suggestion to book a comprehensive metabolic panel.

These suggestions appear as **CTA cards** at the bottom of article and course pages — "Curious about your biomarkers? Book a diagnostic package."

## Premium Discount

Premium members receive a **10% discount** on diagnostic packages. This discount is applied automatically when booking through the platform.
