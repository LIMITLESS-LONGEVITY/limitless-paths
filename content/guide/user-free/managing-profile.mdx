# Managing Your Profile

<RoleTag role="user-free" />

Your PATHS profile stores your account information and preferences. This guide covers how to access and update your account settings.

## Accessing Account Settings

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>
    Click your **avatar** or initials in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2}>
    Select **Account Settings** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step number={3}>
    You will see your profile page with your current account details.

    <Screenshot src="user-free/account-settings.png" alt="Account settings page showing profile details" caption="The account settings page" />
  </Step>
</StepList>

## Updating Your Name

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>
    On the account settings page, locate the **name** fields.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2}>
    Edit your first name and last name as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step number={3}>
    Click **Save** to apply the changes.
  </Step>
</StepList>

## Updating Your Email Address

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>
    On the account settings page, locate the **email** field.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2}>
    Enter your new email address.
  </Step>
  <Step number={3}>
    Click **Save**. You may be required to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.
  </Step>
</StepList>

<Callout type="warning" title="Email verification">
  Changing your email address may require you to verify the new address by clicking a confirmation link. Until verified, your previous email remains active on the account.
</Callout>

## Changing Your Password

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Password requirements and best practices">
    Your password must be at least 8 characters long. For maximum security, use a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Consider using a password manager to generate and store strong passwords. Avoid reusing passwords from other services.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>
    On the account settings page, find the **password** section.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2}>
    Enter your new password. It must be at least 8 characters long.
  </Step>
  <Step number={3}>
    Confirm the new password by typing it again.
  </Step>
  <Step number={4}>
    Click **Save** to update your password.
  </Step>
</StepList>

<Callout type="tip" title="Choose a strong password">
  Use a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. A password manager can help you generate and store strong passwords securely.
</Callout>

## Your Profile Page

Your profile page shows:

- **Your name and email** — The identity associated with your account.
- **Current tier** — Your subscription level (Free, Regular, Premium, or Enterprise).
- **Account creation date** — When you joined PATHS.

</Callout>
