# Upgrading Your Plan

<RoleTag role="user-free" />

Your free account gives you a taste of what PATHS offers. To unlock the full content library, course enrolment, and AI-powered features, you can upgrade to a paid subscription at any time.

## Available Tiers

### Regular

- Full access to the article library across all six longevity pillars
- Course enrolment with progress tracking
- Interactive quizzes and knowledge checks within content

### Premium

Everything in Regular, plus:

- Access to premium-exclusive articles and advanced courses
- AI-powered tutor with **health-aware personalization** (50 conversations/day)
- [Health Profile](/guide/user-paid/health-profile) with biomarker tracking and trend charts
- Personalized [daily protocols](/guide/user-paid/dashboard) (morning, afternoon, evening routines)
- AI-generated [action plans](/guide/user-paid/action-plans) after course completion
- [Telemedicine consultations](/guide/user-paid/telemedicine) included in your plan
- 10% discount on diagnostic packages

### Enterprise

Everything in Premium, plus:

- Organisation-level access for teams
- Multi-user management and reporting
- Custom content and learning paths for your organisation

<Callout type="info" title="Not sure which tier?">
  Most individual users find that **Regular** unlocks everything they need to get started. **Premium** is ideal if you want AI-assisted learning and the deepest content library. **Enterprise** is designed for organisations training teams.
</Callout>

## How to Upgrade

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>
    Click your **avatar** or profile icon in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2}>
    Select **Account Settings** or **Billing** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step number={3}>
    On the billing page, review the available tiers and select the plan that fits your needs.

    <Screenshot src="user-free/billing-tiers.png" alt="Billing page showing available subscription tiers" caption="Compare subscription tiers on the billing page" />
  </Step>
  <Step number={4}>
    Choose between **monthly** or **yearly** billing. Yearly plans offer a discounted rate.
  </Step>
  <Step number={5}>
    Click **Upgrade** to proceed to checkout. Payment is handled securely through **Stripe**.

    <Screenshot src="user-free/stripe-checkout.png" alt="Stripe checkout page" caption="Secure payment via Stripe" />
  </Step>
  <Step number={6}>
    Complete your payment details. Your new tier is activated immediately upon successful payment.
  </Step>
</StepList>

<Callout type="success" title="Instant activation">
  Once payment is confirmed, your account is upgraded immediately. You can start enrolling in courses and accessing premium content right away — no need to log out and back in.
</Callout>

## Billing & Payment

- All payments are processed securely by **Stripe**. PATHS does not store your card details.
- You can manage your subscription, update payment methods, or cancel from the billing page.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain access until then.

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Managing your subscription after upgrading">
    You can change your plan, switch between monthly and yearly billing, update your payment method, or cancel your subscription at any time from the billing page. Navigate to **Account Settings** > **Billing** to access these options. If you cancel, your access continues until the end of the current billing period, and any course progress is preserved.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

<Callout type="warning">
  If you cancel your subscription, your account reverts to the free tier at the end of the billing period. Any course progress is preserved and will be available if you re-subscribe.
</Callout>
