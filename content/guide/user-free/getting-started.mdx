# Getting Started with PATHS

Welcome to **PATHS by LIMITLESS** — your gateway to evidence-based longevity education. This guide will walk you through creating your account and getting set up on the platform.

## Creating Your Account

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>
    Navigate to the PATHS platform and click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner of the page.

    <Screenshot src="user-free/signup-button.png" alt="Sign Up button in navigation bar" caption="Click Sign Up in the top-right corner" />
  </Step>
  <Step number={2}>
    Enter your email address and choose a secure password. Your password must be at least 8 characters long.

    <Screenshot src="user-free/registration-form.png" alt="Registration form with email and password fields" caption="The registration form" />
  </Step>
  <Step number={3}>
    Click **Create Account** to submit your registration.
  </Step>
  <Step number={4}>
    Check your email inbox for a verification email from PATHS. Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</StepList>

<Screenshot src="user-free/email-verification.png" alt="Email verification success page" caption="Your account is activated after clicking the verification link" />

<Callout type="tip" title="Email not arriving?">
  Check your spam or junk folder. If you still don't see it, you can request a new verification email from the login page.
</Callout>

## Exploring the Platform

Once your account is verified, you can:

- **Browse courses** — View all available courses and their descriptions
- **Read free articles** — Access articles available at the free tier
- **Search content** — Use semantic search to find topics that interest you
- **View your profile** — Manage your account settings

<Callout type="info">
  As a free user, you have access to a selection of articles and course previews. To unlock the full library, AI tutor, and interactive quizzes, consider [upgrading your plan](/guide/user-free/upgrading-plan).
</Callout>

## Understanding Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll land on the homepage where you can see:

1. **Featured courses** — Curated courses highlighted by the editorial team
2. **Latest articles** — Recently published longevity articles
3. **Content pillars** — The six pillars of longevity education
