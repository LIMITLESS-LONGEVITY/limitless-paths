# The Six Content Pillars

<RoleTag role="user-free" />

All content on PATHS is organised around six evidence-based pillars of longevity. Each pillar represents a fundamental domain of health optimisation, and every article and course is tagged to one or more pillars so you can focus your learning.

## Nutrition & Metabolic Health

Covers the science of how food affects ageing and long-term health. Topics include metabolic flexibility, glucose regulation, intermittent fasting, micronutrient optimisation, and dietary strategies backed by clinical evidence. This pillar helps you understand the biochemistry behind food choices and their impact on healthspan.

## Exercise & Physical Performance

Focuses on movement as a longevity intervention. Content spans resistance training, cardiovascular conditioning, VO2 max optimisation, mobility, and exercise programming for different age groups. You will find practical, research-backed guidance on how to structure physical activity for maximum lifespan benefit.

## Sleep & Recovery

Explores the critical role of sleep quality and recovery in the ageing process. Topics include circadian rhythm management, sleep architecture, recovery protocols, HRV monitoring, and the biological repair mechanisms that depend on restorative sleep.

## Mental Health & Cognitive Function

Addresses cognitive longevity and psychological well-being. Content covers neuroplasticity, stress management, meditation and mindfulness, cognitive decline prevention, and the interplay between mental health and biological ageing.

## Preventive Medicine & Diagnostics

Covers proactive health screening and early detection. Topics include biomarker tracking, advanced diagnostics (full-body MRI, DEXA, continuous glucose monitoring), genetic testing, and how to interpret lab results in the context of longevity rather than simply disease absence.

## Longevity Science & Anti-Aging

Dives into the frontier research driving the science of ageing itself. Topics include senolytics, NAD+ pathways, telomere biology, epigenetic reprogramming, and emerging therapeutic interventions. This pillar translates cutting-edge research into accessible education.

## How Pillars Appear on the Platform

- **Article cards** display a pillar tag so you can identify the topic area at a glance.
- **Filtering** on the articles page lets you select one or more pillars to narrow your view.
- **Course descriptions** reference which pillars they cover, helping you choose structured learning paths aligned with your interests.
