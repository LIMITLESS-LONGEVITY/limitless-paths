# Course Previews

<RoleTag role="user-free" />

Courses on PATHS provide structured, multi-module learning paths through longevity topics. As a free user, you cannot enrol in courses, but you can preview their full structure to understand what each course offers.

## What You Can See

When you open a course detail page, the following information is available to all users, including free accounts:

- **Course title and description** — A detailed overview of what the course covers and what you will learn.
- **Module outline** — The complete list of modules that make up the course, each with its own title and summary.
- **Lesson titles** — Within each module, you can see the titles of individual lessons, giving you a clear picture of the learning progression.
- **Pillar tags** — Which longevity pillars the course addresses.

<Callout type="info">
  Course previews are designed to give you full transparency into what a course contains, so you can make an informed decision about upgrading.
</Callout>

## What Is Locked

The following course features require a paid subscription:

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Locked features for free users">
    - **Enrolling in the course** — The enrol button is unavailable for free users.
    - **Lesson content** — The actual educational material within each lesson (text, video, interactive elements) is not accessible.
    - **Progress tracking** — Since you cannot enrol, course progress and completion tracking are not active.
    - **Quizzes and assessments** — Interactive knowledge checks within lessons require enrolment.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

## Navigating a Course Detail Page

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>
    From the **Courses** listing page, click on any course card to open its detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2}>
    Read the course description at the top to understand the scope and learning objectives.

    <Screenshot src="user-free/course-detail-header.png" alt="Course detail page showing title, description, and pillar tags" caption="The course detail page with description and pillar tags" />
  </Step>
  <Step number={3}>
    Scroll down to the **module outline** to see how the course is structured.
  </Step>
  <Step number={4}>
    Expand or browse individual modules to review lesson titles and get a sense of the content progression.

    <Screenshot src="user-free/course-module-outline.png" alt="Course module outline showing modules and lesson titles" caption="The module outline lets you preview the full course structure" />
  </Step>
</StepList>

## Choosing a Course

<Callout type="tip" title="Compare before upgrading">
  Review the module outlines of several courses to find the ones most aligned with your goals. This helps you choose the right subscription tier — Regular unlocks the core library, while Premium and Enterprise add advanced content and AI-powered features.
</Callout>
