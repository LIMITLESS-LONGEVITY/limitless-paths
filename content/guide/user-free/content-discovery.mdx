# AI Content Discovery

<RoleTag role="user-free" />

The **Discover** page at `/discover` uses AI to recommend personalized learning paths based on your health goals. Instead of browsing a catalog, describe what you want to improve and let PATHS build a structured path for you.

## How It Works

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Navigate to **Discover** in the main navigation bar.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Type a health goal in natural language — for example, *"I want to optimize my sleep quality and reduce inflammation."*</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click **Discover**. The AI analyzes your goal, searches available content, and generates a structured learning path.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Results appear as a numbered learning path with reasoning for each recommendation.</Step>
</StepList>

<Screenshot src="user-free/discover-page.png" alt="Discover page with AI-Powered badge and suggestion buttons" caption="The Discover page with 6 suggested goals and a free-text input." />

## Suggested Goals

Don't know where to start? Click one of the six pre-built suggestions:

- Optimize my sleep quality
- Reduce inflammation naturally
- Improve cardiovascular health
- Balance my hormones
- Build a longevity routine
- Boost cognitive performance

## Who Can Use Discovery

Discovery is available to all users, but results improve with more data:

| User Type | Experience |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Free (unauthenticated)** | Prompted to sign in |
| **Free (authenticated)** | Basic recommendations from available content |
| **Paid** | Richer recommendations including premium content |
| **Premium with Health Profile** | Most personalized — recommendations factor in biomarkers and goals |

<Callout type="tip">
  For the most personalized discovery results, [set up your Health Profile](/guide/user-paid/health-profile) first. The AI uses your goals, biomarkers, and pillar priorities to rank recommendations.
</Callout>
