# Progress Dashboard

<RoleTag role="user-organization" />

PATHS tracks your learning activity automatically. As you complete lessons, finish quizzes, and work through courses, your progress is recorded and available for you to review at any time.

## Viewing Your Progress

Your learning progress is accessible from your **account page**. Here you can see:

- **Enrolled courses** — all courses you have joined, with completion percentage for each
- **Completed lessons** — a record of every lesson you have finished
- **Quiz results** — scores from quizzes you have taken
- **Enrollment history** — when you enrolled in each course

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Click your avatar or name in the top-right corner of the page.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Select **Account** from the dropdown menu.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Review your enrolled courses and progress indicators.</Step>
</StepList>

<Screenshot src="user-organization/progress-dashboard-account.png" alt="Account page showing enrolled courses with completion percentages and progress bars" />

## Understanding Completion Tracking

PATHS tracks progress at three levels:

| Level | What Is Tracked |
|-------|----------------|
| **Lesson** | Marked complete when you reach the end of a lesson page |
| **Module** | Complete when all lessons within the module are finished |
| **Course** | Complete when all modules are finished |

<Callout type="info">
  Progress is saved automatically. If you close your browser mid-lesson, you can return later and pick up where you left off. There is no need to manually save.
</Callout>

<Accordion title="What your admin can see">
As an organization member, your progress is visible to your organization administrator. This allows them to:

- Monitor team-wide course completion rates
- Identify team members who may need additional support
- Report on training compliance for organizational requirements
</Accordion>

<Callout type="tip" title="Your data stays within your org">
  Only your organization's administrator can view your progress. Other team members cannot see your individual results, and other organizations on the platform have no access to your data.
</Callout>

## Resuming a Course

To continue a course you have already started, go to the **Courses** page or your **Account** page. Courses in progress display a **Continue** button that takes you directly to the next incomplete lesson.

<Screenshot src="user-organization/progress-dashboard-continue.png" alt="Course card with Continue button showing progress percentage and next lesson" />

## Learning Streaks

Your dashboard tracks your **learning streak** — consecutive days with at least one lesson completed. The streak counter and flame icon appear in your stats cards. See [Learning Streaks](/guide/user-paid/learning-streaks) for details.

## Certificates

Completing a course automatically generates a certificate. Your certificates are tracked by your organization for compliance purposes. See [Team Certificates](/guide/user-organization/team-certificates) for details.
