# Accessing Assigned Content

<RoleTag role="user-organization" />

Your organization administrator selects which content is available to your team. This includes articles, courses, and learning paths that align with your organization's training goals. This guide explains how to find and access that content.

## How Org Access Levels Work

Every piece of content on PATHS has an access level — Free, Regular, Premium, or Enterprise. Your organization is assigned an access level by your admin, and you can view any content at or below that level.

<Callout type="info">
  For example, if your organization has **Premium** access, you can view all Free, Regular, and Premium content that has been tagged for your organization's tenant.
</Callout>

## Finding Your Organization's Content

Content assigned to your organization appears throughout the platform just like any other content. There is no separate section you need to navigate to — org-assigned articles and courses appear in the main content listings, search results, and course catalogs.

To identify content available through your organization:

- **Courses page** — browse all courses. Courses within your org's access level display an **Enroll** button.
- **Articles page** — articles available to your org appear in the standard article listing.
- **Search** — use the search bar to find topics relevant to your role. Results respect your combined access level.

<Screenshot src="user-organization/assigned-content-courses.png" alt="Courses page showing available courses with Enroll buttons for organization members" />

<Accordion title="Access level tiers">
| Tier | What You Can Access |
|------|-------------------|
| Free | Publicly available articles and course previews |
| Regular | Core longevity education content and full course enrollment |
| Premium | Advanced content, specialized courses, and AI tutor |
| Enterprise | Complete content library with priority support |
</Accordion>

<Callout type="tip" title="Not seeing expected content?">
  If a colleague can access content that you cannot, your accounts may have different personal tiers. Remember that PATHS uses the higher of your personal tier and org access level. Contact your administrator if you believe your access level is incorrect.
</Callout>

## Enrolling in Org Courses

Enrolling in a course assigned by your organization follows the same process as any other course:

<StepList>
  <Step number={1}>Navigate to the **Courses** page from the main menu.</Step>
  <Step number={2}>Find the course you want to take and click on it.</Step>
  <Step number={3}>Click the **Enroll** button on the course detail page.</Step>
  <Step number={4}>Begin the first lesson. Your progress is saved automatically.</Step>

<Screenshot src="user-organization/assigned-content-enroll.png" alt="Course detail page with the Enroll button and course module overview" />
</StepList>
