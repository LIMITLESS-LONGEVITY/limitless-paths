# Compliance Dashboard

<RoleTag role="user-organization" />

If your organization uses PATHS for staff training, the **Compliance Dashboard** at `/account/team` gives managers a real-time view of team completion rates, certifications, and training status.

<Callout type="info">
  The Compliance Dashboard is only visible to **team managers** (publishers with tenant access) and **admins**. Regular organization members will not see the "Team" navigation item.
</Callout>

## Dashboard Overview

The compliance dashboard includes:

- **Overview cards** — Staff count, total enrollments, certificates earned, overdue training
- **Staff table** — Expandable rows showing each team member's enrollments, progress, and certificates
- **Certificate tracking** — Expiry warnings for certifications within 30 days of expiration
- **CSV export** — Download all staff compliance data for reporting

## Staff Table

The staff table lists all members of your organization with columns for:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Name | Staff member's full name |
| Email | Contact email |
| Enrollments | Number of active course enrollments |
| Certificates | Number of earned certificates |
| Status | Completion status across assigned content |

Click any row to expand and see detailed enrollment and certificate information for that member.

## Certificate Expiry Warnings

If your organization has enabled **certification expiry** (configured by your admin), certificates approaching their expiry date are flagged with a warning badge. This helps managers ensure staff recertify before deadlines.

## Exporting Data

Click the **CSV Export** button to download a spreadsheet of all staff compliance data. The export includes names, emails, enrollment status, certificate numbers, and completion dates — suitable for regulatory reporting.
